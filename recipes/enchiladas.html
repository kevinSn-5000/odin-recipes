Enchiladas Verdes (Suizas) Recipe

Enchiladas verdes, a classical and delicious mexican dish!

The next is a brief but yet great description of this amazing dish. The Enchiladas Verdes is a mexican dish made with fried tortillas, chicken meat, chess, a great green sauce and sometimes accompanied with rice, onions and avocado!

If you have never tasted this meal, you are missing an absolute delicious experience, and a not so hard to cook one!!
But first let's take a look at the ingredients we will need.

Ingredients

  • One onion
  • Oil
  • Avocado
  • Serrano chili 40g
  • cilantro
  • Cream
  • One chicken breast
  • Pepper
  • Chess
  • Salt
  • Garlic
  • Tomatoes

Recipe

  1. First, we boil 500gr of tomatoes, the 40gr of onion, 5gr of garlic, 40gr of the serrano chili and a little cilantro.
  2. Once they are cook, we remove the water and liquate our ingredients to make our delicious sauce for the enchiladas.
  3. Next, we stir-fry the sauce in a previously hot frying pan with oil, and add flavor with salt and pepper at ease. Now we wait for the sauce to cook a little.
  4. For the chicken, we have to cook it in hot water with a piece of onion and let it cook for 15 minutes. Once it's cooked, we let it cool and then we shred the chicken.
  5. Going on with the recipe we have to put the tortillas in warm oil and remove the excess of it once we put them out. We don't want tortilla chips, but neither the raw tortilla, mind that the texture is manageable and resistent.
  6. Now, finally for making the enchiladas, we grab a tortilla, fill it with chicken and give it form by bending the tortilla.
    To let them perfect we pour the sauce all over our enchiladas, all along with the chess, cream, avocado and a twig of cilantro.
    If you wish tou can add some rice by the enchiladas. Now our dish is completed and all that is left is to enjoy our creation!
