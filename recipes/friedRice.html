Fried Rice Recipe

Fried Rice, an easy, nutritious and delicious dish!

This recipe of rice has a good variety of versions, since it can be made with seafood, eggs, or meat. Although it is often used as an accompanying dish, it's equally perfect for being the main dish.
With that said let's jump right to the necessary ingredients!

Ingredients

  • One cup of white rice
  • Two eggs
  • Two bacon rashers
  • Oil
  • One carrot
  • Two shallots
  • Half a cup of frozen peas
  • Soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper

Recipe

  1. First of all we need to cook the rice in a large saucepan of boiling water for 12 minutes, after that we drain and leave to cool.
  2. In a large frying pan over medium heat, we heat oil and add the eggs for two minutes to form an omelette, then transfer the eggs to a separate plate.
  3. Now, we add bacon to wok, keep cooking for 4 minutes, then we add the carrots and after another minute the peas, shallots and rice, after cooking 4 minutes we finally add the eggs and the soy sauce, don't forget to keep string in these whole process of cooking. When the dish is heated and well cooked serve immediately.
    Now that should be it! All that there is left is to enjoy this delicious fried rice!!
