Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Pepperoni Pizza, a complete classical and delicious dish!

The pizza is one of the most famous and favorite food of many people around the world, it has the advantage that it comes in an limitless amount of varierities, with pineapple, different kinds of chesse and meats, vegetables, less or more crunchy, etc.

In this case we will focus on the classical pepperoni pizza, a safe, simple, but yet completely exquisite meal that will leave you asking for more!
Let's take a look at the ingredients for this preparation.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounces pizza dough
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 20 slices of pepperoni
  • 12 ounces mozzarella cheese
  • Flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper

Recipe

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees Â°F
  2. Roll out the dough on a lightly floured surface until it's a 14-inch diameter circle
  3. Now it's time to add the toppings, first the sauce, then the chesse and finally the pepperoni and we season with black pepper
  4. All that is left is to bake the pizza, this will take around 18 to 20 minutes until the crusts are browned and the cheese is bubbly.
